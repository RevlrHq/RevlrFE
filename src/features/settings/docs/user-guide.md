# User Settings Guide

Welcome to the Revlr Settings page! This comprehensive guide will help you customize your account and optimize your event management experience.

## Getting Started

Access your settings by clicking the **Settings** icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner of your dashboard, or navigate directly to `/dashboard/settings`.

## Settings Sections

### 1. Profile Settings

Manage your personal information and account details.

**What you can do:**
- Update your name, email, and contact information
- Upload and manage your profile avatar
- Set your organization details and website
- Configure timezone and language preferences

**Tips:**
- Keep your profile information up-to-date for better networking opportunities
- A professional profile photo helps build trust with event attendees
- Setting your correct timezone ensures accurate event scheduling

### 2. Security Settings

Protect your account with advanced security features.

**What you can do:**
- Change your email address (requires verification)
- View and manage active login sessions
- Revoke access from specific devices
- Monitor account activity

**Security Best Practices:**
- Regularly review your active sessions
- Log out from devices you no longer use
- Use a strong, unique password for your account
- Enable email notifications for security changes

### 3. Notification Preferences

Control how and when you receive updates about your events.

**Notification Types:**
- **Email Notifications**: Event updates, registration alerts, payment notifications
- **Push Notifications**: Real-time alerts for mobile devices
- **In-App Notifications**: Updates within the Revlr platform

**Frequency Options:**
- **Immediate**: Get notified as soon as something happens
- **Daily Digest**: Receive a summary once per day
- **Weekly Summary**: Get a weekly roundup of activities

**Pro Tip:** You can test notifications to see how they'll look before enabling them.

### 4. Interface Preferences

Customize your dashboard and user experience.

**Customization Options:**
- **Theme**: Choose between light, dark, or system-based themes
- **Dashboard Layout**: Select compact, comfortable, or spacious layouts
- **Default Views**: Set your preferred starting views for events and analytics
- **Language & Region**: Configure language and date/time formats

**Accessibility Features:**
- High contrast mode for better visibility
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader compatibility

### 5. Media Provider Settings

Connect external media services for enhanced event visuals.

**Supported Providers:**
- **Unsplash**: Access millions of high-quality photos
- **Pexels**: Free stock photos and videos
- **Pixabay**: Diverse collection of images and graphics

**Managing Connections:**
- Connect new providers through secure OAuth
- View and manage permissions for each service
- Disconnect providers you no longer use
- Monitor connection status and refresh expired tokens

### 6. Data Export

Download your account data for backup or portability.

**What's Included:**
- Profile information and settings
- Event data and analytics
- Registration and attendee information
- Revenue and transaction history

**Export Process:**
1. Select the data types you want to export
2. Request the export (processing may take a few minutes)
3. Receive an email notification when ready
4. Download your data securely

**Important Notes:**
- Export links expire after 7 days for security
- You can request a new export at any time
- Data is provided in standard formats (JSON, CSV)

### 7. Billing & Payments

Manage your payment methods and view transaction history.

**Payment Management:**
- Add, update, or remove payment methods
- Set a default payment method for subscriptions
- View detailed billing history and invoices
- Download receipts for tax purposes

**Subscription Management:**
- View current plan details and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade your subscription
- Manage billing cycles and renewal dates

### 8. Account Management

Control your account lifecycle and data retention.

**Account Actions:**
- View account creation date and statistics
- Understand data retention policies
- Request account deletion (if needed)

**Account Deletion:**
- **Important**: This action cannot be undone
- You'll receive email confirmation before deletion
- Some data may be retained for legal/business purposes
- Active events must be resolved before deletion

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Navigate settings quickly with these keyboard shortcuts:

- `Alt + S`: Open settings page
- `Tab`: Navigate between sections
- `Enter`: Activate buttons and links
- `Esc`: Close modals and dialogs
- `Ctrl/Cmd + S`: Save changes (where applicable)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Settings not saving:**
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have the necessary permissions
- Try refreshing the page and attempting again

**Email verification not received:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Ensure the email address is correct
- Request a new verification email

**Media provider connection failed:**
- Verify your internet connection
- Check if the provider service is operational
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the service

**Export taking too long:**
- Large accounts may take longer to process
- You'll receive an email when the export is ready
- Contact support if it takes more than 24 hours
